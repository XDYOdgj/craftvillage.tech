Earcu 47x17 Grey Bathroom Mat Rug

The Earcu 47x17 grey bathroom mat rug is the perfect blend of comfort, safety, and style for your home. Crafted from diatomaceous earth, it absorbs moisture quickly and dries in no time, keeping your bathroom floor clean and dry. The non-slip rubber backing offers reliable traction on wet surfaces, helping prevent slips and falls in your bathroom, kitchen, or laundry room.

With its low-profile and thin design, this mat is easy to move under doors and around wheels, making it ideal for small spaces or high-traffic areas. Its smooth and non-shedding surface ensures that dirt and hair don't stick, allowing for simple maintenance with just a damp cloth or quick rinse. Whether you're in the shower or walking through the house, this mat adds a modern aesthetic while delivering exceptional functionality.
